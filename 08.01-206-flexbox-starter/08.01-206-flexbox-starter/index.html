I'm Tamara.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found traveling, hiking and searching for new stories.

I was born and raised in Winston-Salem, NC but my real roots reach to Mexico. I owe my artistic ablilites to my mixture of different cultures because, it allows me to express what I am exposed to.

My Passion: I love computers and technology - It is the most innovative and forever changing things that can only get better. So learning to code, I am channeling what I have truly been wanting to do.

A Fun Fact: I actually have about 5 hobbies on top of being a mother. I love to sew, photography, digital drawing, painting, and nails. Usually, I rotate my focus between each of them.
